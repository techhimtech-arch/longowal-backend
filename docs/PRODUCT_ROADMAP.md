# HP Evergreen - Product Strategy & Roadmap

## 🔥 Strong Product Pitch Line
> **HP Evergreen is a structured digital ecosystem for tree plantation, survival monitoring, community participation, and environmental accountability.**

## 🔥 Simple Investor Line
> **Plantation should not end at planting. It must continue until survival.**

## 🔥 Real Product Value
Ye sirf app nahi hai: **It creates measurable environmental accountability.**

---

## 🏗️ Product Users
* **Admin:** Controls full platform
* **Organization:** Manages own plantation
* **Group Leaders:** Manage members
* **Volunteers:** Field activity
* **Sponsors:** View impact

---

## ✅ Phase 1 — Foundation (Minimum Working Product)
**Goal:** System ko live usable banana.

1. **Authentication + Roles (Already mostly ready)**
   * Login
   * User roles
   * Admin permissions
2. **Organization Module**
   * Organizations, departments, villages, institutions (e.g., school, panchayat, NGO)
3. **Group Module**
   * Groups create (e.g., Mahila Mandal, Youth Club, School Eco Club)
4. **Plant Catalog**
   * Plants master data (species, local suitability, season)
5. **Plantation Event**
   * Track (event date, location, organizer, plant count)
6. **Tree Registration**
   * Every planted tree entry (tree id, species, planted by, location)

**Outcome:** Platform usable ho jayega 🌱

---

## ✅ Phase 2 — Monitoring Layer
**Goal:** Ped survive kare aur monitoring aaye.

1. **Tree Health Tracking**
   * Alive / dead, growth stage, remarks
2. **Tree Photo Upload**
   * Every tree proof (photo, upload date)
3. **Geo Tagging**
   * Tree exact location (latitude, longitude)
4. **Inspection Module**
   * Assign inspection tasks.

**Outcome:** Ab product real environmental platform banega.

---

## ✅ Phase 3 — Community Participation Layer
**Goal:** Villages ko active banana.

1. **Volunteer Module**
   * Volunteer register (villagers, students, groups)
2. **Assignment Module**
   * Assign (watering, inspection, maintenance)
3. **Attendance / Participation Logs**
   * Track who participated.

**Outcome:** Community ownership create hogi 👥

---

## ✅ Phase 4 — Supply & Nursery Management
**Goal:** Plant supply chain control.

1. **Nursery Module**
   * Track nursery stock, available plants
2. **Plant Requests**
   * Village demand (plant request, approval)
3. **Supply Dispatch**
   * Track issued plants.

**Outcome:** Inventory control aa jayega 🌿

---

## ✅ Phase 5 — CSR / Sponsor Layer
**Goal:** Funding and partnerships.

1. **Sponsor Module**
   * Companies onboard (sponsor name, contribution)
2. **Sponsor Plantation Mapping**
   * Show sponsor planted where
3. **Reports**
   * Automatic CSR reports

**Outcome:** Funding-ready product 💰

---

## ✅ Phase 6 — Analytics & Intelligence
**Goal:** Decision support.

**Dashboard:**
* Total trees
* Survival %
* Village wise plantation
* Organization wise impact

**Future AI:**
* Survival prediction
* Low survival alerts
